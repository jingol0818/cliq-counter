Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Cliq ("we", "our", or "the app") is a tally counter and tasbeeh application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Counter names, values, and settings
  • Count history and statistics
  • App preferences (theme, sound, vibration settings)

This data is stored only on your device using local storage and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Google Account (Optional)

If you choose to use the Google Drive backup feature (Pro), we request access to your Google account solely to store and retrieve your backup data in your personal Google Drive. We do not access any other files in your Google Drive.

3. Advertising

The free version of Cliq displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • General location data
  • Ad interaction data

Pro users do not see any advertisements.

4. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by Google's payment infrastructure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as described above (Google AdMob for advertising, Google Drive for optional backup).

6. Data Security

All app data is stored locally on your device. Backup data is stored in your personal Google Drive account, protected by Google's security measures.

7. Children's Privacy

Cliq is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
